Car Jackings are on the Rise

Insights into the crimes that are affecting everyday people.

Car Theft is taking over the Washington, D.C. area.

As car jackings are rising around the nations capital. It is impossible to ignore the constant threats that drivers are facing in broad daylight and after dark. This article will provide updated statistics and safety precautions for all DMV drivers.

930 Carjackings Since the Start of 2023

Since the 2023 New Year, over 900 carjackings have taken place in the nations capitol as of December 11, 2023 according to the Metropolitan Police Department.The MPDC have also released a statement claiming that of the 930 carjacking reports, only 167 resulted in an arrest. And a shocking 62% of the charged cases were committed by juveniles. According to theDistrict of Columbia Sentencing Commission's "2022 Violent Offenses Data Response," carjackings have increased by since 2018. This year the numbers have doubled since 2022.

"62% were juveniles."

On Dec. 11, Mathew Graves, The U.S. attorney for the District of Columbia, stated that 10 teenagers were charged as adults in a string of carjackings across Washington, D.C. The MPDC alleges they are responsible for 15 vehicle thefts and reportedly held their victims at gunpoint. One of the arrested teenagers allegedly shot a ride-share driver multiple times but did not end up succesfully taking the vehicle. This is the largest federal carjacking arrest in D.C. of the year.
